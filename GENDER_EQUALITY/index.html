5 GENDER EQUALITY Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls.
Respect The major part of gender equality is respect, as are some attitudes, ideas, and justifications. As Nationalians, we have built a culture of disregard for women and persons of all gender identities. And also their right to self-determination and equality.


The learner is knowledgeable of all forms of gender inequality, violence, and discrimination (such as harmful customs like female genital mutilation, honour killings, and child marriage, unequal employment opportunities, pay, and language construction, traditional gender roles, and gendered impacts of natural hazards), and they comprehend both the contemporary and historical causes of gender inequality.
