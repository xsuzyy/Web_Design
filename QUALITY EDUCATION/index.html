The students of NU Laguna views education as a public benefit, a worldwide common good, a basic human right, and a foundation. And also they are able to appreciate the value of their own skills in terms of bettering their lives, particularly in terms of employment and entrepreneurship.

Peace Justice and Strong Institutions

SDG 16 advocates for peaceful, inclusive societies built on the rule of law, respect for human rights, and care for the most vulnerable members of society. Additionally, it envisions institutions that are open, efficient, and accountable, support non-discriminatory laws and policies, fight corruption, bribery, and organized crime, and stop violence, terrorism, and crime.
SDG 16 asks for decision-making that is responsive, inclusive, participatory, and representative, with poor nations playing a larger role in institutions of global governance.
Assessing SDG 16 in an EU context focuses on advancements made in promoting access to justice, ensuring peace and personal security, and raising trust in EU institutions.
